ABOUT PROTEIN ANTIGENICITY PREDICTION

Understanding Proteins

Proteins are complex molecules that perform vital functions in all living organisms. They are made of amino acids arranged in specific sequences that determine their 3D structure and function. Some proteins can trigger immune responses (antigenic) while others don't (non-antigenic).

FASTA Format

The FASTA format is a text-based format for representing nucleotide or protein sequences:

>identifier [optional description]
SEQUENCE

Example of a valid FASTA format:

>sp|P0DTC2|SPIKE_SARS2
MFVFLVLLPLVSSQCVNLTTRTQLPPAYTNSFTRGVYYPDKVFRSSVLHSTQDLFLPFFS

How Our System Works

Our AI-powered system predicts protein antigenicity using:

  1. UniRep Embeddings: Converts protein sequences into numerical representations
  2. Multiheaded CNN: Deep learning model that analyzes sequence patterns
  3. Immune Response Prediction: Identifies antigenic regions that may trigger immune responses
